About

Grounded in hardware, driven by systems thinking

A brief look at how I work and where my attention naturally goes.

I'm an Electronics and Communication Engineering student with a practical, build-first approach to problem solving. My background spans circuit design, embedded systems, and system integration, with real-world testing across IoT, robotics, and drone engineering.

Alongside hands-on prototyping, I bring project coordination and team leadership experience — I've helped organize workflows, lead small technical teams, and carry projects from a rough sensor breadboard to a working, demo-ready device.

My work consistently returns to one theme: taking an everyday safety or monitoring problem and solving it with an inexpensive microcontroller, the right sensor, and a clear-headed system design.
